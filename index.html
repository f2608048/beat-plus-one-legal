BEAT+1 Privacy Policy

Effective date: 17 July 2026

Developer: AppLab

Privacy contact: f2608048@gmail.com

Summary

The clean version of BEAT+1 has no account system, contains no advertising SDK, requests no Internet permission, and does not transmit personal or sensitive user data to the developer.

Data processed on your device

The app stores game statistics such as best score, game count, wins, chain length, and day streak in local Android storage. Challenge codes contain a score and tap timing data. A temporary PNG result card may be created in the app cache when you request it.

Install referrals

Google Play may provide an install-referrer string containing a BEAT+1 challenge code and campaign labels. The app reads this string on the device so the shared challenge can open after installation. The clean app does not send the referrer to the developer.

Sharing

Nothing is shared automatically. When you select a share action, Android shows its system share sheet and sends the challenge text or image only to the app or person you choose. The privacy practices of the selected third-party app are governed by that party.

Children

BEAT+1 is not designed or marketed for children under 13. The clean app knowingly collects no personal data from any user.

Retention and deletion

Local statistics remain until you clear the app's storage or uninstall it. Share-card files are temporary cache files that Android or the app may remove. Because the developer receives no clean-app user data, there is no remote user profile to delete.

Security and changes

The clean app minimizes risk by keeping game data on-device. This policy may be updated when functionality or legal requirements change; the effective date above will be updated.
